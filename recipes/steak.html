Steak

Description

Some really good steak.

Easy to make and only takes 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs New York Strip Steaks (2 steaks)
  • 1/2 Tbsp avocado oil or other high heat cooking oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper, freshly ground
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and quartered
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Dry steak with paper towels then season with salt and pepper
  2. Heat up the pan then add the oil when it is hot. Let the oil sit on medium heat until it shimmers, then add steak
  3. Cook each side for 4 minutes on each side, flipping however often you want
  4. When both sides are done, reduce heat to low and add the butter, garlic, and rosemary to the pan. Then baste the steak for a minute by spooning the butter onto the steak
  5. Transfer steak to cutting board and loosely cover and rest for about 10 minutes before serving
