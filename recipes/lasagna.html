Lasagna

Description

Some really good lasagna.

Easy to make and only takes 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • other stuff

Steps

  1. Mix beef, onion, and garlic
  2. Add all the tomato stuff into it
  3. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake with foil for 25 minutes then for another 25 minutes without the foil
